Who are we ?

Cafe Heaven, The largest cafe chain in Sri Lanka has won the LMD customer service online survey for the third time from cafe and coffee sector for rendering an excellence customer service to its beloved customers.Roaming the bustling streets of East London, soaking up culture, meeting wonderful people and tantalizing the taste buds with world food, it was these experiences that spurred us to create our very own downtown cafe-space.
